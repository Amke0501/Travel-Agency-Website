Our Services

Meridian Travel Services offers a B2B and B2C integrated back office to its clients, consumers or members who are either corporate or leisure travellers.
This outlines our investments into our call centre infrastructure ensuring that comprehensive and sophisticated processes are continually implemented.
Our services are not available to the general public and we only assist our business partners adding a level of exclusivity to our discerning travellers.

Our Vision

At Meridian Travel Services our vision revolves around service excellence and our commitment to ensure the success of our clients. To this end we strive to maintain our unrivalled reputation, grow our relationships with our long-term partners, invest in our talented staff and always look out for new and exciting ways to deliver the best possible products. This is what puts MTS a cut above the rest.

Goal

Our constant research into new developments within the IT sector makes provision for travel services, ensuring ease of access for our partners. By constantly being on the lookout for the latest technological advances in the travel industry, we open up a host of opportunities for our clients and service suppliers. We constantly seek solutions that help our partners offer exceptional standards of service, faster transactions and enhanced operational efficiency.

Success

Through our continuous negotiations with our industry partners we are able to offer our clients the best possible service and pricing. Meridian Travel Services only partners with reputable industry suppliers covering destinations worldwide.
